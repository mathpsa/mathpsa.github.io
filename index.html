Problems

Low Grades
Students recieve low grades in Math and Science.
Students Fail Class
Students don't recieve enough credits for graduation and can't enter college
Parents get mad
Parents and students become sad and develope mental health issues.

Purpose

• Help students achieve higher grade in Math.
• Make it easier for students to achieve higher grade in Math
• Make students more engaged in Math class
• Make parents more involved in their student's education
• Make students and teachers more connected

Facts

We Survey 19 freshman, softmore and juniors. Around 42% of the students that we surveyed are currently failing their current Math class. Most students think their teacher can improve by focusing on the steps to solving a problem instead of giving a solution. Many other students also feel that the class is going to fast and they don't have time to understand all the material. For students, they can improve their involvment in class. They should also spend more time studing at home. Teacher and parents should actively reach out to each other to make sure their students is on track.
Other students also think teacher need to change their ways of teaching. Here is a direct quote from a current Math 3 student. "Teachers should change their ways of teaching, For example my teacher openes up the big ideas book and asigns an assignment and prays we get it. This year he also set us up to fail, he was apsent for the first 3 weeks. When he came back he want so fast, so fast Where if you were apsent, you were in deep trouble. I feel like math teahcers could be more connected with the studnts. I feel like some of them forget that we have an outside life."
